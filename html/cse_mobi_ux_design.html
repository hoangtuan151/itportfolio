About

This course is optimized for the developer who is looking to efficiently learn the most important design techniques that will help them make better apps (with a focus on mobile/Android).

I've Learned

  • How to thinking (at high-level) as a designer when designing an application's UX;
  • How to work as a designer in practice: from persona creating to low-res wireframing and to mockup designing;
  • How to use some design tools: Prezi, Balsamiq, Sketch (at basic level).
